Quick Introduction to Carrot

Carrot is yet another PHP framework. It is experimental in nature. Carrot's core libraries depends heavily on anonymous functions and mandates the usage of dependency injection container. Carrot as a framework consists of a front controller, a default router, a dependency injection container, and an error/exception handler. You can say that it's a sort of micro framework.
